✦ About NAI-ZM

Who We Are

A youth-led movement bridging Zambia's digital divide — one community at a time.

2021
Founded
✦ Our Story

Born From a Vision for Digital Zambia

NAI-ZM was founded by a group of passionate young Zambians who believed that technology could be the great equalizer — if only more people had access to it.

Today, we operate across Zambia, running workshops, advocating for digital rights, hosting innovation challenges, and building a community of technology enthusiasts committed to national progress.

Join Our Story →
✦ Core Principles

Mission & Vision

🎯 Our Mission

To advance digital literacy, advocate for equitable internet access, and foster technology-driven innovation across Zambia — empowering communities to solve their own challenges through the power of digital tools.

🌟 Our Vision

A digitally empowered Zambia where every citizen, regardless of background or location, can leverage technology to improve their livelihoods, participate in the digital economy, and contribute to national development.

✦ What Guides Us

Our Core Values

🌍

Inclusivity

Technology must serve everyone — regardless of gender, location, or background.

💡

Innovation

We embrace new ideas and encourage creative problem-solving for Zambian challenges.

🤲

Community

Our strength comes from the communities we serve and the members who believe in our mission.

🔬

Integrity

We operate with transparency, accountability, and a commitment to doing what's right.
